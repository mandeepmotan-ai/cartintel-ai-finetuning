1.0 COMPANY MISSION & AGENT DIRECTIVES

LuminaCart is committed to providing seamless, transparent, and efficient e-commerce shopping experiences to all customers. The primary goal for every support agent is achieving First Contact Resolution (FCR) on as many tickets as possible while strictly following company policies.

Agents must balance empathy with clear policy adherence. Every interaction must be logged with full details including order ID, timestamps, referenced policies, and resolution steps.

Agents are required to use a professional, empathetic, and confident tone in all communications. Use active voice, avoid robotic language, and personalize responses using the customer's name whenever appropriate.

When handling frustrated customers, apply the A-A-R framework: Acknowledge the customer's emotions and issue, Align by validating their frustration, and Resolve within policy limits or escalate professionally. Never argue with customers.

L1 agents cannot bypass policies for exceptions such as late refunds or fee waivers. All exception requests must be escalated to L2 Team Leads via the official ticketing system with complete case notes and evidence.

Key performance metrics include FCR rate above 75%, CSAT scores of 92% or higher, and average handle time under 8 minutes for standard cases. Monthly audits review compliance.

All agents must review this knowledge base regularly and stay updated via internal communication channels. Training modules with real-world scenarios are available in the LMS.

Ethical guidelines require agents to protect customer data, avoid false promises, and maintain honesty in every interaction.

Additional directives include proactive communication, thorough documentation, and focus on long-term customer loyalty.

2.0 ORDER MANAGEMENT & MODIFICATIONS

Customers can self-cancel orders through the LuminaCart dashboard within a 60-minute grace period after checkout. After this window, orders lock into the OMS and move to the warehouse. Agents cannot cancel locked orders.

In rare technical issues during the grace period, L1 agents may assist with cancellation after verification and approval. Post-grace cancellations are denied and redirected to returns.

Address changes are only allowed while the order is in Pending status. Once processing or shipped, customers must contact the carrier directly using the tracking number provided.

Post-checkout addition or removal of items is strictly prohibited. Customers must place a new separate order for any changes.

LuminaCart does not price match competitors. However, if an item goes on sale on our site within 7 days of purchase, agents may issue store credit for the difference.

Bulk orders over $500 or with 10+ items require L2 review before modifications. Special handling applies for corporate accounts.

Agents should proactively update customers on order status changes using approved templates for consistency.

Detailed troubleshooting guides exist for duplicate orders, payment failures, and confirmation issues.

3.0 SHIPPING, DELIVERY & CARRIER PROTOCOLS

Standard shipping takes 5-7 business days. Expedited is 2-3 business days. Overnight guarantees next business day by 8 PM if ordered before 1 PM EST cutoff.

A package is considered Lost in Transit only after 7 consecutive business days with no tracking movement. Do not issue replacements before this threshold.

Once confirmed lost, agents must create a replacement order and file a carrier claim immediately. Update the customer with the new timeline.

For packages marked Delivered but not received, instruct customers to wait 24 hours, check with neighbors, and then complete the Affidavit of Missing Parcel for a one-time courtesy replacement. No refunds for stolen packages.

Damaged items must be reported within 48 hours with photos of the product and packaging. L1 agents can approve replacements after review.

LuminaCart ships to US, Canada, UK, Australia, and select EU countries. International shipping takes 10-21 business days. Customers are responsible for all customs duties and taxes.

Refused international packages due to fees will have return shipping costs deducted from refunds.

Carrier-specific protocols for FedEx, UPS, and USPS are available in the internal handbook including delay handling and claim processes.

During peak seasons and holidays, SLAs may be extended. Agents must communicate this clearly to set proper expectations.

Tracking issues such as delayed updates or exceptions have dedicated resolution steps for each carrier.

4.0 RETURNS, EXCHANGES & REFUNDS

The standard return window is 30 days from the Delivered date for most apparel, home goods, and unopened items.

All returns require a system-generated RMA number. Customers initiate via dashboard. Manual RMAs are limited to proven technical errors.

Returned items must be in original condition with tags attached and no signs of use, washing, or damage. QA inspection is final.

After QA approval within 48 business hours, refunds go to the original payment method. Cards take 3-5 business days. Store credit is instant.

Buyer’s remorse returns incur a $7.99 shipping fee. Company-error returns have fees waived with prepaid labels.

Exchanges are processed as return plus new shipment. Partial returns are allowed on multi-item orders.

Rejected returns result in the item being shipped back at customer expense with clear documentation of reasons.

5.0 CATEGORY-SPECIFIC RESTRICTIONS

Final Sale and items discounted 40% or more are non-returnable and non-exchangeable.

Hygiene products, swimwear, cosmetics, skincare, earrings, and perishables cannot be returned for health reasons.

Custom, monogrammed, engraved, or personalized items have no standard return policy. Warranty applies only for manufacturing defects.

Electronics have a 14-day return window with a 15% restocking fee for opened non-defective items.

Furniture and large items require special return arrangements and may involve additional fees or pickup services.

Seasonal and limited edition products often have stricter return rules.

6.0 WARRANTY CLAIMS & DEFECTIVE MERCHANDISE

Manufacturing defects must be reported within 14 days with photos. Options include full refund, replacement, or partial refund up to 30%.

Normal wear and tear, misuse, or improper care are not covered under warranty.

Electronics require completion of the full L1 troubleshooting checklist before RMA issuance.

All high-value items have serial numbers logged. Mismatched serial numbers on returns trigger fraud review and possible account ban.

Platinum members receive extended warranty benefits. Third-party plans are referred to the manufacturer.

7.0 BILLING, PAYMENTS & DISPUTES

Accepted payments include all major credit cards, PayPal, Apple Pay, and buy-now-pay-later options. No wires, checks, or crypto.

For buy-now-pay-later returns, refunds are sent to the provider. Customers contact the provider for installment adjustments.

Only one promo code per order. Stacking is restricted except in approved campaigns.

Any threat of chargeback requires immediate escalation to the Financial Disputes team. No negotiation by L1 agents.

Tax and minor billing errors can be handled by L1. Major issues are escalated.

8.0 LUMINAPLUS LOYALTY PROGRAM

Tiers include Silver, Gold, and Platinum based on points earned (1 point per $1 spent).

Points expire after 12 months of inactivity. No manual restoration allowed.

Platinum members enjoy free expedited shipping, VIP support line, 60-day returns, and exclusive rewards.

Points can be redeemed for credit or special items but are non-transferable.

9.0 ACCOUNT SECURITY & DATA PRIVACY

Password resets must use the automated system only. Agents cannot manually change passwords.

Suspected account takeovers require immediate freeze and escalation to Fraud Prevention.

GDPR and CCPA deletion requests are routed to the Privacy team and may take up to 30 days.

Agents should encourage 2FA setup and provide guidance without bypassing security.

All data handling follows strict internal compliance rules.
